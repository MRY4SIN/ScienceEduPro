Famous Professors and their discoverings


Mathematics:

Andrew Wiles

Known for proving Fermat's Last Theorem, a long-standing mathematical problem.

Terence Tao

Renowned for his contributions in harmonic analysis, partial differential equations, and number theory.


Biology:

Rita Levi-Montalcini

Discovered nerve growth factor (NGF), crucial for the growth and maintenance of nerve cells.

James Watson & Francis Crick

Known for discovering the structure of DNA, the double helix model.


Physics:

Albert Einstein

Famous for his theories of relativity and the equation E=mcÂ², revolutionizing our understanding of space and time.

Richard Feynman

Renowned for his work in quantum mechanics and the development of Feynman diagrams.


Chemistry:

Marie Curie

Pioneer in radioactivity and the first woman to win a Nobel Prize in two scientific fields.

Linus Pauling

Known for his work on the chemical bond and awarded Nobel Prizes in Chemistry and Peace.
